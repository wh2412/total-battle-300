Our Clan rules

As a member of 300, please follow our rules:

  1. We expect you to be active and achieve a minimum of 200k might per week
  2. Deliver minimum 10 crypts level 10 and higher and 2 citadels to the clan per day
  3. Send resources / caravans to all clan buildings (read more in chat 300ReadOnly)
  4. Use clan speed ups daily whenever you see a clan process
  5. Teleport to the clan territory within 3 days, if you are a new member
  6. Always check your journal and the clan chat - and please participate.
  7. Notify superiors if you'll be absent for more than 3 days, otherwise you will be expelled
  8. Send all gold bars to the Kingdom
  9. Participate in clan tournaments and events
  10. Follow the RoE (Rules of Engagement). If you are not familiar with them - please read them carefully HERE
