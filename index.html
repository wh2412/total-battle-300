History

In ancient Sparta, a select group of warriors known as the 300 stood as the ultimate symbols of strength, discipline, and bravery. Handpicked by King Leonidas, these soldiers were chosen for their unmatched combat skills and deep loyalty to their homeland.

The story of the 300 has become a legendary testament to resistance and sacrifice. Their heroic stand at Thermopylae remains one of history's most defining moments, embodying a tale of extraordinary valor that continues to inspire defenders of freedom to this day. The legacy of the 300 endures as a powerful symbol of courage and unwavering determination.

Our Clan rules

As a member of 300, please follow our rules:

  1. We expect you to be active and achieve a minimum of 200k might per week
  2. Deliver minimum 10 chests and 2 citadels to the clan per day
  3. Send resources / caravans to the all clan buildings (read more in chat 300ReadOnly)
  4. Use clan speed ups daily whenever you see a clan process
  5. Teleport to the clan territory within 3 days, if you are a new member
  6. Always check your journal and the clan chat - and please participate. Respond to private messages from superiors
  7. Notify superiors if you'll be absent for more than 3 days, otherwise you will be expelled
  8. Send all gold bars to the Kingdom - you only need 10 for the daily jobs
  9. Participate in clan tournaments and events
  10. Follow the RoE (Rules of Engagement). If you are not familiar with them - please read them carefully HERE
  11. We don't accept any attacks on cities in K151 outside the CoT
  12. Be polite and respectful
